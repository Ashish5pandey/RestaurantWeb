About Us

Welcome to GMPP – The Brand of Ashish!

Established in 2024, GMPP (Great Meals Perfect Place) was born out of a passion for bringing vibrant, bold flavors to the heart of Andhra Pradesh. From our humble beginnings, we set out with a simple mission: to create a dining experience that celebrates the rich culinary heritage of the region with a modern twist.

Our Story

Located in the bustling city of Noida, GMPP (Great Meals Perfect Place) quickly became a local favorite. Our founders, Devendra Singh, Vikash Rai, and Ashish Pandey, envisioned a place where people could gather to enjoy not just a meal, but a culinary adventure. Every dish we serve is crafted with care, using the finest locally-sourced ingredients and traditional cooking techniques.

Our Cuisines

At GMPP, we pride ourselves on offering a menu that caters to every taste bud:

  • Signature Spicy Dishes: Dive into our renowned dishes like the Spicy Andhra Veg/Chicken, a fiery blend of local spices and tender chicken, or our Chili Paneer, which combines perfectly crisp paneer with a spicy, tangy sauce.
  • Heavenly Biryani: Experience the rich flavors of our Hyderabadi Biryani, slow-cooked with aromatic spices and fragrant basmati rice, a true feast for the senses.
  • Authentic Curries: Our menu features an array of curries, from the Andhra Mutton Curry to the Veg Chettinad, each offering a unique flavor profile that embodies the essence of South Indian cuisine.
  • Freshly Made Breads: Enjoy our selection of freshly baked naans and rotis, perfect for soaking up our delicious gravies.
  • Sizzling Starters: Begin your meal with our Masala Dosas or Stuffed Mirchi Bajji, guaranteed to set the tone for a memorable dining experience.

Our Commitment

At GMPP, every meal is prepared with love and passion. Our chefs are dedicated to ensuring that every dish not only meets but exceeds your expectations. We’re committed to providing a warm and welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family. Our mission is to provide the best dining experience with fresh ingredients and exceptional service.
